Getting Started with IoTLogIQ
Administrator Dashboard

This tutorial’s goal is to demonstrate the basic usage of the most popular IoTLogIQ’s Administrator Dashboard features. You will learn how to:

  • Create customers, locations, areas, assets, devices
  • Assign devices to different assets.
  • Create Users.
  • Assign Users’ roles and permissions
This Dashboard can only be accessed by super-admin users
1-Select the desired company, location, area, and assets.

1-In the top section of the dashboard, you'll find access to the different entities: companies, locations, areas, and assets that are available in each of the different sub-sections.

-When you select one, the sub-sections will load in the following column.

-Within each panel, you will be able to add, edit, or delete items in the category.

2-In the bottom section of the dashboard you can view all the locations and assets available
2-You can click on each of these assets to get a list of all items available on that section.
2- Create a new device
You can start creating a new device by clicking on the icon on the top right section of the dashboard.
This will open the following Pop Up where you'll be able to select the name, type, and device label.
Add, edit and delete buttons are available in all entities: customers, location, areas, assets and devices
3- Assign the device
To assign a device, click on the icon located on the top right section of the dashboard.
This will open the following Pop Up where you'll be able to select the area or machine and the devices that will be assigned.
4-Create a new User
Create a new User by clicking on the icon on the top right of the dashboard.

Complete the User’s information, select their role, and assign their permissions.

 

Depending on the user role selected, the available permissions will differ:

 

-Viewers: cannot perform CRUD operations.

-Supervisors: can edit and delete most of the entities.

-Admins: have access to all actions.

This will create an activation link that can be sent to the account owner. After setting their email and password they will gain access to all the features available for the account.
5-Log In
As a super administrator, you will be able to log in as any user of your company and see their dashboard and devices available to them.
Once logged in you’ll be redirected to another dashboard where you can access all the User’s information.
You can access all information about the devices by clicking on the details button on the Device section.
Depending on the device profile, you will be redirected to a different dashboard with different specifications and graphics about its measurements.
